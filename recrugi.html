Who We Are

A next-generation recruitment consultancy transforming how companies hire and talent grows.

RecruGI Pvt Ltd is a next-generation recruitment consultancy founded in 2025 in Vijayawada.

We help businesses hire smarter while empowering job seekers with career guidance, interview preparation, resume building, recruitment training, and AI-driven hiring strategies.

Our mission is connecting talented people with growing companies through AI-powered recruitment and personalized hiring solutions. We put people before processes and partnership before placement.
